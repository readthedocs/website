Pricing FAQ

Do I need a credit card for the trial?

No. Start your 30-day trial without one — we'll only ask if you decide to keep going.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, change plans anytime. Monthly plans are pro-rated; annual plans renew at the new tier.

Do you offer annual pricing or purchase orders?

Yes. Annual plans are available on every paid tier and can be paid by invoice or purchase order.

What about open source projects?

Open source documentation is always free on Read the Docs Community.
